Return Policy

At Delicacy, we want to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. Please read our return policy carefully:

Receiving returns

- Directly at the store address, Hotline 0981 604 832, Fanpage (Please contact the Hotline for quick returns and exchanges)

- Time: 8am - 7pm (Complaints after business hours will be resolved the next day)

Return conditions

- Provide product images and videos within 24 hours of receipt for fresh fruits and vegetables, 2 hours for semi-processed products

- Product has Dilicacy's purchase invoice, labels and packaging intact (for packaged goods)

- Products are stored properly

- Return policy does not apply to complimentary products or promotions > 20%

Exchange and refund regulations

1. Product innovation

- The store will be responsible for exchanging a new product if the fault is caused by the seller (damage, wrong quantity, expired warranty,...)

- In case of exchange due to the customer's subjective opinion (change of mind, unsightly product, etc.), the customer needs to bring the goods to the store to be exchanged.

2. Refund

- 100% refund for fruit damaged by the seller's error and the store will be responsible for recalling the product.

- 85% refund if the customer wants to return the product due to a change of mind and the customer will bear shipping costs.

- Refunds will be made via bank transfer according to the account information you provide within 24 hours.
